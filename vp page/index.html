Vice Presidential Candidates

HIGHLIGHTS: Pence v Harris Vice Presidential debate








Where do Harris and Pence stand on climate change?
Harris once supported the Green New Deal with Senator Bernie Sanders, but she recently has shifted her position to promote Biden's climate plan, which requires the American government to re-join the Paris Climate agreement and hopes to reduce U.S. carbon emissions significantly. In contrast, Pence used to promote the philosophy that climate change is a myth, but he has since altered his stance to support the idea that human activity does influence climate change in some way.
Where do Harris and Pence stand on COVID-19?
Pence has headed the White House Coronavirus Task Force since February and continues to vary his stance on the severity of the pandemic, but he has been working to decrease the number of cases in the United States. Harris, on the other hand, continues to condemn the way that Pence and Trump have tackled the pandemic, promoting the idea that she has Biden will enforce stricter regulations to bend the curve.
Where do Harris and Pence stand on criminal justice reform?
Pence is a strong supporter of police forces, backing Trump's notion of law and order and disagreeing with efforts to defund the police. Harris, however, supports economic and educational improvements to minority communities to help combat police brutality.
Where do Harris and Pence stand on healthcare?
Harris supports the expansion of health care access for American citizens, while also preserving private health insurance. Pence, in contrast, supports Trump's goal to revoke Obamacare and turn over the efforts of the Obama-Biden administration, essentially defunding public healthcare.
Where do Harris and Pence stand on gun control?
Pence is a public advocate of the second amendment and the National Rifle Association, opposing potential restrictions and condemning gun control efforts. Harris, on the other hand, proposes reforms to counter gun violence by creating bans on assault weapons and expanding background checks.
