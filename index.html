Comparing the Candidates

Joe Biden vs. Donald Trump

in the 2020 presidential election

On November 3, the general election will decide the next American president. The candidates in the race are Former Vice President Joe Biden (the Democratic nominee) and incumbent president Donald Trump (the Republican nominee). Amidst the pandemic and ample social change, the nation has never been more polarized by two presidential candidates. As the two political sides shift farther away from one another, Democrats and Republicans fight for the promotion of their views. Recently, the candidates faced off in a live debate for the nation and Trump shortly after became diagnosed with COVID-19. Since then, there have been no further debates between the two candidates, though their vice presidential picks (Democrat Kamala Harris and Republican Mike Pence) faced off last week.
